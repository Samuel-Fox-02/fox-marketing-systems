# 3 Welcome Flow Mistakes That Are Costing You Subscribers (And Sales)

The welcome flow is the most‑opened email sequence you'll ever send. Open rates of 50‑60% are common. Yet many businesses treat it as an afterthought. Here are three costly mistakes and how to fix them.

## Mistake 1: One and Done
Sending a single “Thanks for subscribing!” email and then going silent for weeks is a waste of peak engagement. Your welcome flow should be at least three emails, spaced over 5‑7 days. This is your chance to educate, build trust, and make a first offer while the subscriber is actively paying attention.

## Mistake 2: No Clear Next Step
Every email in the flow should have a single, obvious call‑to‑action. Don't ask them to follow on social, browse your store, and read a blog post in the same email. Pick one action per email. The first email's job is to deliver the lead magnet. The second email's job is to tell a story. The third email's job is to present an offer.

## Mistake 3: Ignoring SMS Opt‑In
Welcome emails have sky‑high engagement. This is the perfect moment to ask for a phone number. A simple “Want early access to restocks and exclusive offers? Join our text list.” can grow your SMS list by 15‑20% of new subscribers.

**Quick fix:** Review your welcome flow right now. Does it have at least three emails? Is each one clear on the next step? Have you asked for SMS permission? Fix the biggest gap first.
